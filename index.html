Kendriya vidyalaya no.2 N.H.P.C Banbasa

I study in a Kendriya vidyalaya.It is the second Kendriya Vidyalaya in the region.It is located in the city of Banbasa near my home town Tanakpur in the champawat district of Uttarakhand state of India.

You can see its location on top. The principal of my school is Mrs. Rangana Barfal.My school from class 1st to class 12th.

Campus life

In my school there is an ordinary timetable like other schools,we have games and art period on thursday and friday.And there is a fun day every saturday in which there are only periods of art,games and music (only till 6th class).On that free period we go to play on the big playground of our school.But many times,Students of big classes like 9th and 12th have dominated most of the playground so we just play in a corner of the feild or sit down and talk.
